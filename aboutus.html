About Us

Liz Becker (they/he):

I am a part-time student in my final year of the iSchool program! I currently live in Chicago with my partner and our two cats - Twiggy and Cat Stevens. My background is in Art History and I love spending time at the Art Institute here in the city. Please feel welcome to connect with me on LinkedIn.
